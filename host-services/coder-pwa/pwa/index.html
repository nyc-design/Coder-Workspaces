Install Coder

Add Coder to your home screen so workspaces open in a full-screen app instead of a browser tab.

Chrome / Edge

Checking install availability…

iPad / iPhone Safari

  1. Tap the Share button at the top of Safari.
  2. Scroll and choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add — the Coder icon appears on your home screen.

macOS Safari

  1. Open Safari's File menu.
  2. Choose Add to Dock….
  3. Confirm — Coder appears in your Dock and Applications.

Requires Safari 17+ (macOS 14 Sonoma or newer).

Already installed

Launch Coder from your Dock, home screen, or apps list. If you uninstalled and want to reinstall, clear this site's data in your browser settings first and reload this page.

Once installed, launching the app goes straight to coder.tapiavala.com.
